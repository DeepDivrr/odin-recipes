Description

A perfect dessert for any gathering, big or small. It is highly customisable which makes it great for all communities. Great conversation starter, and has time-warping capabilities never seen before in other dishes!

Ingredients

  • A handful of content
  • 100g of brown sugar
  • One account
  • Some paid actors
  • A cliff

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 150 degrees C
  2. Mix one content with 10g of brown sugar, then start preparing the stage.
  3. After 10 minutes, take the mixture out and place it straight in front of the stage, forcing it to sing a song or perform anything else of the sort. Make sure your paid actors mock the mixture as violently as possible.
  4. After 10 minutes, allow the mixture to rest for another 15 minliutes. Then, place the created cookie inside a cookie cutter, making sure it has no way of escaping. Commit to take the cookie to a cliffside.
  5. Take the cookie to the cliffside, and push it into the river down below.
  6. Nearly always, the cookie will revert time to the closest moment before inevitable death. Make sure to stay alert for this moment. When you see the cookie cutter in your hands again, make sure to keep your hands steady.
  7. Your cookie will dislike you, and it may even develop mental health conditions. Make sure that you check in with a professional to ease its pain. Make sure to specify that you only meant to teach it the time-warping capabilities it has.
  8. Your dessert is ready. Make sure it consents to being eaten, otherwise the known space-time thing will collapse in on itself due to a strong inherent repellent force within the cookie
